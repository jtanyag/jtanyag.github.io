About Justin

I'm a full-stack developer who completed LEARN Academy's full-stack JavaScript coding bootcamp in San Diego, CA. I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with my Bachelor's degree in Psychology.

After years in customer service roles, my interest in tech was piqued during my time spent in a Quality Assurance position at a small company that created and maintained websites for auto dealerships. I began teaching myself to code through free online courses when I realized that this may be the career field for me. After finding out about LEARN Academy's full-stack JavaScript web development program, I decided to take the first steps into an exciting and rewarding career.
